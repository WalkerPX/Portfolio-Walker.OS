About

Walker Birchfield

History Graduate & Creative Director

Hello and thank you for visiting my webpage. My name is Walker Birchfield and I'm excited to share my portfolio with you.

I graduated from Purdue University in May 2026 with a bachelor's degree in History, a minor in IT, and a certificate in Cornerstone. Throughout my time there, I developed a deep passion for research, development, digital media, creative content, and sports. It's a combination that continues to drive my work today.

I currently work for Purdue Athletics in the Ross-Ade Brigade, the official student section for Purdue Football, where I serve as Creative Director. Previously, I worked for the Lafayette Aviators, a summer collegiate baseball team competing in the Prospect League, also serving as Creative Director.

I am currently in the application process for law school, with top options including Michigan State University, Indiana University–Indianapolis, and Indiana University–Bloomington. I hope to use my law school education and sports background to continue working in athletics, focusing on sports law or player agency.
